1.2.3 Dig
Friday, November 18, 2022
1:48 PM
Another DNS Recon tool

Example:

\> **dig pmc.sc**

Limit it to the name servers:

\> **dig pmc.sc NS**

Display all the Records

\> **dig pmc.sc ANY**

To look for Mail Servers

\> **dig pmc.sc MX**
